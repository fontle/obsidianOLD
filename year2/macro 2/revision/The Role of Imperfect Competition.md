(economic output and unemployment) can be adjusted by aggregate demand changes alone.

Imperfect competition is needed in creating the rationality constraint on both the consumers 
and firms. In imperfect competition, the firm's maximises profit where marginal revenue is 
equal to marginal cost, consequently the price is above the marginal costs and therefore there 
is an excess supply of output. In this scenario labour's budget constraint is not binding, as the
