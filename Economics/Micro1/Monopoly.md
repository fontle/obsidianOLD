## Revenue
---
### Total Revenue

$$r(y)=p(y)\times y$$

### Marginal Revenue

$$
MR(y)=\frac{\partial r(y)}{\partial y}
$$

## Profit Maximisation
---
The profit maximisation quantity and price occurs where marginal revenue is equal to marginal cost: 

$$
\max [p(y)\times y] \equiv MR(y) = MC(y)
$$
