**LM relation** shows the Equilibrium in the money market is defined where money demand is equal to money supply.

$$ 
\begin{align*}

\text{Money Demand}:& \\
M_d&= \underbrace{\$Y}_{\text{Nominal Income}}\cdot \overbrace{L(i)}^{\text{Nominal Interest Rate}}\\

\text{Equilibrium}:& \\
\frac{M}{P}&= Y\cdot L(i)

\end{align*}
$$
