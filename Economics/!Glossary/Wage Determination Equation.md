The wage determination relation shows how nominal wages are calculated.

$$
W=P^{e}\cdot F(u,z)_{-,+}
$$

- $W$ is nominal wages
- $P^{e}$ is the expected price level
- $F$ is a function of unemployment ,$u$, and institutional factors, $z$, such as trade union power.
